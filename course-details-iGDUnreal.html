Game Development with Unreal Engine

Course Description

The Game Development with Unreal Engine program by ISCS is designed to take you from a beginner to a confident game developer with a strong command of Unreal Engine. This hands-on course guides you through the key principles of 3D game creation, Blueprints, C++ scripting, environment design, and performance optimization over a structured 8-month journey.

You’ll build a portfolio of polished game projects while mastering workflows used by AAA studios. The program is ideal for students, professionals, and creatives looking to break into or level up in the game development industry using Unreal Engine.

What You Will Learn
  • Unreal Engine interface, project setup, and asset management.
  • Blueprint visual scripting and C++ integration.
  • 3D environment design, lighting, and animation techniques.
  • Game physics, player mechanics, and collision systems.
  • Optimization, packaging, and cross-platform deployment.
Learning Outcomes
  • Create fully functional games using Unreal Engine 5.
  • Develop gameplay mechanics using Blueprints and C++.
  • Design immersive environments with realistic lighting and effects.
  • Debug and optimize game performance for various platforms.
  • Build a professional game development portfolio.
Why Choose Us?
  • Guidance from Unreal Engine experts with industry experience.
  • Access to cutting-edge tools and lab facilities.
  • Blended learning with real-world projects and feedback.
  • Career development support and mentoring.
How to Enroll?
  • Visit the ISCS website and fill out the online application form.
  • Submit your personal statement and (if applicable) portfolio.
  • Attend a virtual or in-person admissions interview.
  • Receive your confirmation and begin your Unreal journey.
Program Duration

The program runs for 8 months and includes a mix of instructor-led sessions and independent project work.

Who is This For?
  • Beginners with a passion for 3D games and interactivity.
  • Developers looking to transition into Unreal Engine development.
  • Artists and designers aiming to bring their visions to life in real time.
Medium

Blended learning model – in-person and online, supported by an LMS for self-paced study and community support.

Certification

Graduates will receive a professional certificate in Game Development with Unreal Engine from ISCS, validating their expertise and readiness for the industry.
